CoolCrew — Product Plan

Features and modules for the field service workforce platform — explained in plain English.

View Design Screens →

1. Overview

CoolCrew is a software solution designed to help AC repair and field service companies run their daily operations smoothly. The entire system is built as two simple frontends connected to a single secure database:

  1. A Mobile App for Workers in the field (designed for easy tap-and-go use on mobile phones).
  2. A Web Dashboard for Managers in the office (designed for desktop computers to manage scheduling and oversight).

We guarantee three core benefits to your business:

  • Accurate Check-Ins: Every check-in and check-out is tied to a verified GPS location and time, along with an optional selfie photo, so you always know your team is where they need to be.
  • Full Tool Accountability: Any tools or materials that leave your office stay registered to the worker's name until they are officially returned and checked back in.
  • Never Lose Data: The mobile app works offline. If a worker has poor signal inside a building, the app saves everything and automatically syncs it once signal returns.

2. Worker Mobile App

This is the clean, simple app that your field technicians will use on their phones. It is designed with big, easy-to-tap buttons and high contrast so it is clearly readable outdoors under bright sunlight.

Signing In & Security

  • Password-Free Log In: Workers simply enter their phone number and receive a secure text message code to log in. They don't have to remember complicated passwords.
  • Quick Daily Access: After the first login, they can open the app quickly using a simple 4-digit PIN.

The Home Screen (Today's Work)

  • Big Check-In Button: A single large button to "Check In" at the start of the day and "Check Out" at the end of the day.
  • Workday Clock: A live timer that shows them exactly how many hours they have worked today.
  • Your Next Job: A prominent card showing their next assigned job with customer details, the address, and easy buttons to start GPS navigation or call the customer.
  • Quick Numbers: Easy counters showing jobs completed today, hours worked, and tools currently checked out.

Timecards & Attendance

  • Verified Location: When workers check in or check out, the app logs their exact map location and takes an optional selfie to verify their identity.
  • Security Checks: The app automatically checks if GPS services are turned off or if someone is trying to fake their location, alerting the manager on the dashboard.
  • End-of-Day Reminders: When checking out, the app shows a summary of their day and warns them if they still have tools checked out or unfinished jobs.

Job Management (My Assigned Calls)

  • Today's List and Map: Technicians can view all their scheduled jobs on a simple list or see them pinned on a map.
  • Job Details: Tap on any job to see the customer's contact info, special access notes, and the brand, model, and age of the AC units on-site.
  • Simple Progress Buttons: They tap "Start Driving" when they leave, "Arrived" when they reach the customer, and "Complete" when they finish.
  • Service Checklist: A step-by-step list of tasks (e.g., "Clean filters", "Check gas pressure") that they must check off.
  • Work Log & Customer Signature: They can easily attach before-and-after photos, write notes, specify any parts used, and collect the customer's signature right on their screen.

Toolbag & Inventory Accountability

  • What You're Carrying: A live screen showing exactly what tools and parts are currently checked out in the worker's name.
  • Checking Out Tools: At the office, workers use their phone to search or scan barcodes of the tools they are taking, select the quantity, and confirm checkout.
  • Returning Tools: At the end of the day, they select which tools they are returning, write off consumed parts (like screws or copper pipes), or report lost/damaged tools with a reason.
  • The Holding Rule: Tools remain marked as "pending return" on the worker's record until they are officially returned and checked back in.

My History & Settings

  • Attendance History: A personal calendar showing which days they worked, their check-in/out times, and map locations.
  • Tool Ledger: A full history of all tools they have checked out and returned over time.

3. Admin Office Dashboard

This is a secure website that managers and business owners use on their office computers to schedule jobs, track attendance, manage inventory, and run the daily business.

Daily Overview

  • Daily Status: See numbers at a glance — who is checked in, how many jobs are finished, and what tools are currently out.
  • Worker Locations Map: View the last-known location of workers on a map, captured only when they update a job status or check in/out (no real-time live tracking).
  • Important Notifications: Instant alerts for things that need immediate attention, such as unassigned emergency jobs, late check-ins, or overdue tools.

Attendance & Payroll Timesheets

  • Daily Punch Log: A clean table showing each worker's clock-in time, clock-out time, total hours, and check-in photos.
  • Verified Maps: Click any clock-in to see the exact map location and selfie to verify they were on-site.
  • Payroll Reports: Easily download weekly or monthly timesheets for all workers as an Excel or PDF file with one click.

Worker Directory

  • Employee Records: A searchable directory of all technicians, their contact details, roles, and branch assignments.
  • Add New Employees: Onboard a new worker in seconds by entering their name and phone number. This automatically sends them a text invite to download the app.
  • Simple Actions: Deactivate past employees instantly or reset a worker's secure login PIN if they forget it.

Scheduling Board

  • Calendar Board: A visual calendar with columns for each worker to schedule and assign jobs.
  • Create a Job Card: Enter client details, addresses, scheduled times, priority levels, task checklists, and required tools.
  • Track Status: Watch the status change as the technician starts driving, arrives, and finishes the work.
  • Quick Adjustments: Easily move a job to a different time or a different worker if plans change.

Clients & AC Unit History

  • Customer Book: A list of all customers, their site locations, and service contracts.
  • AC Equipment Register: For every client, track all AC units on-site (brand, model, capacity, installation date, and warranty status).
  • Complete Service History: See a clear historical timeline of all past service visits, including technician notes, photos, parts used, and signatures.

Inventory & Tool Reconciliation

  • Stock Levels: Keep a catalog of tools, spare parts, and consumables (like gas or filters), showing how many are in the office, how many are checked out, and get alerts for low stock.
  • Who-Holds-What: See exactly which worker has which tools, how long they've had them, and flag any items that are overdue.
  • Reconciliation Screen: Confirm returned tools, write off parts used at jobs, or log damaged or lost tools with clean reasons.

Business Reports

  • Attendance Summary: View total hours worked and on-time stats per worker or branch.
  • Productivity Reports: See how many jobs each technician completed and their average job duration.
  • Tool Costs & Loss: Track which tools are frequently lost, damaged, or written off to control shrinkage and costs.

System Settings

  • Security Permissions: Set who can edit schedules, view customer info, or delete data (Field Workers, Managers, and Super Administrators).
  • Branch Offices: Easily manage multiple office locations or regions.
  • Business Rules: Configure standard working hours, check-in radius restrictions, and default job templates.
  • Audit Trail: A secure log of important manager actions (like writing off lost inventory or deleting records) to keep operations transparent and honest.

4. Marketing Role (Leads)

A new role for marketing people to manage client leads.

Mobile App (Marketing Login)

  • Leads List: View a simple list of client leads with names and phone numbers.
  • Add Lead: Add new client leads by entering their name and phone number.
  • Lead Details: Tap a lead to view details and call them directly from the app.

Admin Dashboard (Manager View)

  • View Leads: Managers can see all client leads added by the marketing team.

5. Key Journeys (How It Works)

A. Daily Worker Flow

The technician opens the app in the morning → Checks in (the app records their GPS and optional selfie) → Views today's job list → Selects the first job and taps for navigation → Arrives at the customer's site and marks "Arrived" → Completes the checklist, logs any parts used, takes photos, and gets the client's signature → Marks the job "Complete" → Repeats this for the next jobs → Checks out at the end of the day (the app warns them if they still have tools).

B. Tool Accountability Flow

At the office in the morning, the worker checks out required tools using the app → The tools are marked as "pending return" under their name on the dashboard → After completing their jobs, they return to the office and log the returned tools on their app → The manager confirms the return on the dashboard → The tools go back into stock, and the worker's pending count goes to zero.

C. Scheduling Flow

An office manager logs into the dashboard → Sees which workers have open slots → Creates new jobs and drags them onto the workers' schedules → The workers receive instant notifications on their phones with the customer's details and address.

D. Onboarding Flow

The manager adds a new employee to the dashboard → The system sends a welcome text to the worker's phone → The worker downloads the app, signs in with their phone number, sets a secure 4-digit PIN, and is immediately ready to receive jobs.

6. Delivery Phases

We build and deliver the system in simple, clear phases. At the end of every phase, we will demonstrate a working part of your product:

  • Phase 1: Log-In & Setup: Setting up the secure database, worker roles, and secure phone-based log-in.
    *You can log in as a worker on a phone and as a manager on a computer.*
  • Phase 2: Attendance Tracker: Building the worker's clock-in screen with map verification and selfie, and the manager's attendance dashboard.
    *Workers can clock in from the field; managers can see exactly when and where they did it.*
  • Phase 3: Job Scheduling: Building the manager's visual scheduling board and the worker's job list, task checklist, and work log (photos and signature).
    *Managers can assign jobs; workers can run through them on their phones and complete them.*
  • Phase 4: Tool Accountability: Building the inventory database, the worker's checkout and return screens, and the manager's approval page.
    *You can track exactly who has which tools and ensure everything is returned at the end of the day.*
  • Phase 5: Reports & Management: Building timesheet exports, PDF reports, and general business settings.
    *You can download attendance timesheets, track tool costs, and manage permissions.*
  • Phase 6: Final Polish & Launch: Testing offline capabilities, securing all data, resolving bugs, and releasing the app to the app stores.
    *The system is live and ready for your team.*

7. Project Assumptions & Scope Boundaries

To ensure a fast and simple delivery, we have designed the project scope based on the following standard business rules. If you need any of these changed, please let us know:

  1. Single Office Operations: The system is designed to run for a single main office (no multi-branch office selector in current scope).
  2. Optional Selfies: Check-in selfie photos are optional for technicians when clocking in or out.
  3. Major Tools & Spares Only: Inventory tracking covers larger tools and main spare parts only (we will not track minor items like individual screws, bolts, or tape).
  4. Technician Self-Checkout: Technicians use the mobile app to check tools out themselves when leaving the office.
  5. No Customer Notifications: Customer-facing text messages (like "technician on the way") are not included in the current scope.
  6. Simple Timesheet Exports: The system does not calculate complex payroll or overtime automatically; it exports standard timesheets to Excel for easy payroll processing.
  7. Android Mobile App: The worker mobile app will be developed for Android mobile phones.
